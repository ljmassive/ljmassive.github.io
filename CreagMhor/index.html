About Creag Mhor Apartment

The apartment was recently redecorated and is a spacious two-bedroom first floor maisonette, which comfortably sleeps four, perfect for couples and families. The whole of the apartment is yours to use for the duration of your stay. Whether you are here for a weekend-break or for a month, Creag Mhor Apartment is your home-away-from-home; with all bedding and towels provided, washing and kitchen facilities, and entertainment system with streaming services available for cozy evenings in. Outside the property there is a communal close, drying green and lawned area.

Creag Mhor Apartment is located in the heart of the picturesque village of Aberfoyle, in The Trossach's, Scotland's first National Park. It is ideally situated for easy access to all amenities - bars, restaurants, shops, play park, golf course, etc. - and just minutes away from the fabulous Queen Elizabeth Forest Park which surrounds the village, where you can walk, fish, cycle to your hearts content. The Aberfoyle 18 hole golf course is a 5 minute drive and the Lake of Menteith Fishery just 5 miles away.
